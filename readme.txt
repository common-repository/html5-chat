Html5 chat is a WP plugin to quickly add webcam video chat to your wordpress.

== Description ==

Html5 chat plugin is quick plugin to help you to embed html-chat.com video chat into your wordpress blog.
Activate the plugin: you will automatically receive the password to your email.
Just insert the short code into your page or your post and you have an audio and video chat into your blog.


== Installation ==

* activate the plugin
* insert the [HTML5CHAT width=100% height=640px] short code into your page or post
* you can configure your chat look&feel through your "configure chat" link
* To make the chat fullscreen; use height=fullscreen. Ex: [HTML5CHAT width=100% height=fullscreen]
 
 
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= How do I change the width and height of the chat ? =
 
you can change the size by editing the [HTML5CHAT width=100% height=640px] tag.

= How do I to set chat in full screen ? =

you can change the size by editing the [HTML5CHAT width=100% height=fullscreen] tag.
 
= How can I moderate my chat =
 
You need to login to your chat through your WP admin. If you are the admin of your WP blog, then you are automatically the admin of the chat.

= How do I change the look and feel of my chat =

You can do that inside your config panel: you can inject some css, change the way html5 works
